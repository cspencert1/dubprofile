FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Dub Profile really free?

Yes. The Free plan is free forever and includes everything you need to publish a profile and start sharing your links. Upgrade only if you want advanced features.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. Dub Profile is fully visual — pick a template, add your links, and publish. If you can use social media, you can build a profile.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. Free and Pro profiles live at dubprofile.com/yourname, and the Business plan lets you connect a custom domain like links.yourbrand.com.

Can I sell products from my profile?

Absolutely. Add products, tips, and digital downloads, and accept payments directly from your page.

Will my profile work on every device?

Every Dub Profile page is responsive and loads fast on phones, tablets, and desktops — no extra work needed.
